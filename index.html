Salem is a Junior studying Computer Science, with an Astrophysics minor at the University of Michigan. Growing up in the Mission District of San Francisco, they were immersed in beautiful art and culture, exposed to the excitement of the bleeding edge of technology. Since then, they’ve taught themself to code, and co-founded a robotics team, a non-profit and a poetry club.

Salem is an artist in all they do, from programming to writing and painting and exploring nature. They have expertise in data analysis, robotics, and machine learning, with research experience in space weather, inflatable moon rovers and musical motion-capture robots.

Nothing excites them more than a good challenge and a good team. They are joining the ranks of young people trying to transform and study the world.
